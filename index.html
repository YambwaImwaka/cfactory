About The Content Factory

Zambia's premier content production company, proudly producing podcasts like Zambia's biggest podcast (That Zed Podcast), social media content and commercials for TV, Radio, and Digital platforms.

Our Mission

At The Content Factory, our mission is to empower voices and amplify stories by producing high-quality content. We provide creators, brands, and thought leaders with the tools, expertise, and a platform to bring their ideas to life—efficiently, creatively, and authentically.

Our Vision

To be a leading hub for innovative video and audio storytelling, where every voice has the power to inspire, inform, and spark change across the world.

Why Choose Us?

  • Producers of the most-followed podcasts in Zambia
  • State-of-the-art equipment for any podcasting need
  • Trusted by major brands in banking and insurance
  • Team of passionate storytellers and technical experts

Our Expertise

With a team of skilled media professionals and a fully equipped production studio, The Content Factory provides end-to-end production solutions, from concept development and recording to editing, distribution, and live broadcasting.
